About us

Bipin Tattoo Ink and Hair Saloon has been in business for over 5 years in Pokhara City. Bipin Chhantyal (owner) has witnessed the tattoo industry evolve over the course of more than 16 years of experience.

We pride ourselves on listening to our clients and incorporating their ideas into a tattoo perfect work of art that will last forever.Bipin's Tattoo & Hair saloon provides its clients with a wide range of innovative designs and crafts that are made in a lawless manner.

At Bipin's Tattoo and Hair Saloon customers feel invited and agreeable and leave with a splendid bit of craftsmanship.
